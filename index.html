About Our Jam

Started in 1985, the Växjö Bluegrass Jam has been bringing together musicians from all over Sweden. We celebrate the rich tradition of bluegrass music through weekly informal jam sessions where players of all skill levels can learn, share, and perform together.

Whether you're a seasoned picker or just starting out, our community welcomes you to join in the music and fellowship.
